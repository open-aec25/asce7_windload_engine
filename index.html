Notes & Limitations

Scope: This prototype produces MWFRS (Main Wind Force Resisting System) pressures for ASCE 7-16 Directional Procedure Chapter 27. Prototype roof scope is limited to flat and gable roofs.

Geometry: Building length and width are fixed architectural dimensions. ASCE L is measured parallel to the selected wind direction and B is measured perpendicular to it, so their derived values change between orthogonal wind cases without changing the building geometry.

Stories: Number of stories means occupied floors only and does not include the roof level. ASCE height h is derived from story/eave height; gable roofs steeper than 10 degrees use mean roof height, while gable roofs at 10 degrees or less use eave height.

Out of scope: Components & Cladding loads, monoslope roofs, mansard roofs, hip roofs, and automatic dual-orthogonal-direction analysis are deferred to future phases.

Disclaimer: This tool is for preliminary estimation only. A licensed structural engineer must review, verify, and stamp all wind load calculations for use in design and construction documents.
